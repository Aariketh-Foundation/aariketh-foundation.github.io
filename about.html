ABOUT Aariketh

Everyone, from the foothills of the Himalayas, lustrous greens of the Tarai and to the streets of Kathmandu valley, deserves updated information, better education, good health, quality living, serene environment and better livelihood.

Aariketh Foundation, works to achieve the vision of everyone everywhere towards equitable, empowered, resilient society. Continuing on this vision and building on our legacy of collaborative research, Aariketh will focus on building the evidence-base needed to address implementation and policy gaps in equity, accessibility, and better quality in education, healthcare, environment through adaptation of innovative technology and help make a resilient society.

Innovative research is important for continuous improvement in promoting quality of life of individuals and providing exploration of novel strategies to optimize access, cost, and quality of living for all. We will continue to root our work in underserved and hard to reach places, where there is both great need and interest for upliftment.

Vision

Everyone everywhere towards equitable, empowered , resilient society

Mission

Banish hurdle for a prosperous society by uplifting ,inspiring , empowering and connecting people

Goal

Strengthen the potential of the vulnerable for poverty reduction thus, contributing towards a sustainable society

Values

Excellence, integrity, diversity, inclusion, collaboration, professionalism
