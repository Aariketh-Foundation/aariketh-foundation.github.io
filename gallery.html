About us

Aariketh Aariketh Foundation, works to achieve the vision of everyone everywhere towards equitable, empowered, resilient society. Continuing on this vision and building on our legacy of collaborative research, Aariketh will focus on building the evidence-base needed to address implementation and policy gaps in equity, accessibility, and better quality in education, healthcare, environment through adaptation of innovative technology and help make a resilient society.

  • Innovative research is important for continuous improvement in promoting quality of life of individuals and providing exploration of novel strategies to optimize access, cost, and quality of living for all. We root our work for underserved and hard to reach places, where there is both great need and interest for upliftment.
