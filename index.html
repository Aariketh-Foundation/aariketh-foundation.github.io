What We Do

We at Aariketh, focus on women,children,persons with disabilities, marginalized and deprived grass root communities to help alleviate poverty by enabling people and making them self resilient.

Where We Work

The recent pandemic has had an unprecedented impact on the direct livelihood of the people all over the world and it still continues to pose a big risk for the future.This has even more severely affected those who were already vulnerable and from marginalized communities. There is a great need to support and uplift those communities and develop self resilient skills. Nepal remains one of the poorest countries in South Asia with 25.2 % percent of the population below the poverty line (ADB,2010). COVID-19 related disruptions have affected livelihoods and resulted in the decline in the economic condition of the country. It has been found that this has affected the poor, and the vulnerable disproportionately, with an expectation that poverty is very likely to increase even more. Additionally, the lack of adequate national budgets to support fundamental needs such as good health and better coverage has resulted in a greater need for non governmental/non profit organizations to step in. Aariketh strives to serve as the support unit to help these unreached and underserved communities to become self resilient and reliant with the goal to “Strengthen the potential of the vulnerable for poverty reduction thus, contributing towards a sustainable society”.

Our Causes

HUNGER AND POVERTY

We hope to reduce hunger and poverty by eliminating childhood malnutrition and promoting children's right to education for those whose families cannot afford it. Additionally, we also focus on improving financial opportunities and choices, developing job-related skills, and investing in education.

EDUCATION AND TRAINING

Aariketh believes that everyone should have equal access to a high-quality education, and it aims to close the gaps in education by increasing teacher capacity, implementing innovative technology, and improving access to education for vulnerable and marginalized populations.
DONATE NOW

DISASTER RISK REDUCTION

Natural disasters endanger more than 80% of Nepalese. It poses a significant threat, particularly to those who are already vulnerable and struggling to make a living. We intend to raise disaster preparedness, emergency response awareness and provide the necessary support.
DONATE NOW

HEALTH AND WELBEING

Nepal faces huge gaps in access to quality health services and infrastructure as well as lack of awareness regarding disease prevention, hygiene and nutrition. We aim to promote awareness as well as provide basic medical services at low to free cost for those who cannot afford and access those services.

About us

Aariketh Foundation, works to achieve the vision of everyone everywhere towards equitable, empowered, resilient society. Continuing on this vision and building on our legacy of collaborative research, Aariketh will focus on building the evidence-base needed to address implementation and policy gaps in equity, accessibility, and better quality in education, healthcare, environment through adaptation of innovative technology and help make a resilient society.

Innovative research is important for continuous improvement in promoting quality of life of individuals and providing exploration of novel strategies to optimize access, cost, and quality of living for all. We root our work for underserved and hard to reach places, where there is both great need and interest for upliftment.
